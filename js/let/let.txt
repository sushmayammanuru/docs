what is let ?
	let keyword was introduced in ES6 (2015).
	it is one of the variable type.
	it can't be redeclared.
	It must be declared before usage.
	re-assignment is possible.
	It has a blockScope.
Why let is introduced in ES6 ?
	 variable defined with var type can be re-declared.
	 re-declaring the variable is wrong approch.
	 to over come re declaration ,let keyword was introduced.
